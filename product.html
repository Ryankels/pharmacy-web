**CardioRelief** Dosage Instructions

Always consult a physician before use. General guidelines:

  1. Take one **5mg** tablet in the morning with a full glass of water.
  2. Do not crush or chew the tablet.
  3. If a dose is missed, take it as soon as you remember, unless it is near the time of the next dose.
